WHO AM I

Hello everyone, my name is HALIMATULSYAHIRAH BINTI SHARIN, but I prefer to be called Syerah. I am currently studying for a Diploma in Library Management (semester 4) at UiTM Kedah. I come from Melaka, and being far from home has helped me become more independent. In April 2025, I officially turned 20 years old. I am the youngest in a family of six.

MY DREAMS AND GOALS

As a Library Management student, my dream is to contribute to the evolution of modern libraries — not just as physical spaces, but as inclusive, digital- friendly knowledge hubs.I want to help make information more accessible, especially to underrepresented communities.

One of my goals is to build a career in information services where I can support users in navigating digital resources and promote lifelong learning. I also aim to improve my skills in metadata, digital archiving, and user experience.

Personally, I want to live a balanced life valuing both productivity and peace. I want to travel more, capture sunsets, and journal my journey as I grow.

MY STRENGTHS IN 3 WORDS

Organized – I love keeping things in order, whether it’s my planner, notes, or digital files.

Calm – I stay composed under pressure and prefer resolving things quietly and thoughtfully.

Creative – I enjoy journaling, decorating, and thinking of new ways to present ideas.
